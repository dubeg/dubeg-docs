# Archiving
## tar cvf   <...>.tar     dir/
## tar cvfz <...>.tar.gz  dir/
## tar cvfj <...>.tar.bz2 dir/
c - create archive
v - verbose (list files)
f - filename of archive

z - filter the archive through gzip
j - filter the archive through bz2

* .tgz = .tar.gz
* .tb2, .tbz = .tar.bz2

# Extracting
## tar xvf  <...>.tar
## tar xvfz <...>.tar.gz
## tar xvfj <...>.tar.bz2
x - extract files form archive
